Patients

Using our system, patients are capable of doing the following:

  1. Registering and logging in.
  2. Editing their user and patient details.
  3. Viewing all their prescriptions, collected or not.

Doctors

Using our system, doctors are capable of doing the following:

  1. Registering and logging in.
  2. Editing their user and doctor details.
  3. Making prescriptions and viewing them.
  4. Viewing all patients in the system.
  5. Viewing drugs being sold in pharmacies.

Pharmacists

Using our system, pharmacists are capable of doing the following:

  1. Registering and logging in.
  2. Editing their user and pharmacist details.
  3. Viewing all prescriptions made.
  4. Making dispensations and viewing them.
  5. Viewing all patients and doctors in the system.
  6. Viewing their pharmacy's inventory.

Supervisors

Using our system, supervisors are capable of doing the following:

  1. Registering and logging in.
  2. Editing their user and supervisor details.
  3. Editing their pharmacy's details.
  4. Adding and viewing their pharmacy's inventory.
  5. Viewing their fellow supervisors and pharmacists working in their pharmacy.
  6. Viewing the contract that they are supervising.
  7. Viewing all pharmaceutical companies and their drugs.

Pharmaceutical Companies

Using our system, pharmaceutical companies are capable of doing the following:

  1. Registering and logging in.
  2. Editing their user and company details.
  3. Creating new contracts, viewing and editing them.
  4. Adding new drugs in supply, viewing and editing them.
  5. Viewing all pharmacies in the system.
  6. Viewing supervisors listed in the company's contracts with pharmacies.
